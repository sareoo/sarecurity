About Me

On the blue side of infosec

Hey! I'm Danny, a 20-year-old Information Security professional with hands-on experience in configuring and maintaining enterprise detective, preventive, and directive controls.

I was unknowingly introduced into this career in my early years through Scambaiting, an internet vigilantism to counter advance-fee fraud, tax scams, technical support scams, pension scams, and financial fraud. Although this was entertainment, this inspired me to direct my path towards cyber security from a young age.

After completing my apprenticeship, I have continued to work in the financial sector to help protect my company's systems and information as an Information Security Analyst.

My apprenticeship focused on cyber risk management; how organisations effectively define, assess, treat, and monitor cyber risks.
